About Us

We a team that wants to improve the medical sector in Rwanda through the use of easy-to-navigate software to create a system whereby everybody's medical history is digitally recorded on a medical I.D, so as to reduce at least 10% of deaths caused by unknown medical history and also improve and transform the way care is delivered and compensated. This I.D. would be accessible to all hospitals and medical professionals.

Why you Choose Us.

Rwanda is currently facing a lack of digitization in the medical sector. This lack of digitization has led to an inconsistency in the recording of patients' medical histories. Not knowing a patient's medical history has had dire consequences for the country, including easily preventable deaths and wrongful handing over of medicine.

TRwanda’s future in health will be driven by technology. Technology has reduced geographical barriers and access to healthcare, diagnosis, and treatment have improved greatly while clinical research has set the trend for evidence-based health decisions..

Although Rwanda still faces significant challenges in meeting its development goals, one of its major successes has been the healthcare sector. Rwanda is one of the few developing countries in the world to have universal health coverage; its community-based health insurance programme – known as Mutuelle de Santé – reaches more than 90% of the population.

  • Patients are the stakeholders at the center of digital medicine, and we also recognize the critically important role of caregivers.
  • Clinical care providers remain at the center of medicine and are ultimately the experts who drive decision making at the point of care.
  • Funders — both government and non-governmental funding organizations that support health research will have significant impact on the direction and pace of the evolution of digital medicine.
